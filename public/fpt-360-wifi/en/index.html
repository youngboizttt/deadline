FAQs

1. Is FPT 360 WiFi a broadband product? 1. Is FPT 360 WiFi a broadband product?

No, FPT 360 WiFi is an AI-driven, smart WiFi service. It works with any Broadband provider by plugging directly into your hub. Additional pods are spread throughout the home to extend coverage to WiFi blackspots.

2. How is FPT 360 WiFi better than a standard WiFi extender/booster? 2. How is FPT 360 WiFi better than a standard WiFi extender/booster?

FPT 360 WiFi adapts to your usage through network improvements. It runs more than 10 different types of improvements that work in the background 24/7, so you get fast and consistent WiFi everywhere. For example, if a change occurs that would impact your WiFi performance, like an increase in connected devices or interference from a neighbor’s WiFi, FPT 360 WiFi will make the necessary adjustments to maintain your WiFi experience.

3. Can FPT 360 WiFi also protect my connected IoT devices like my doorbell or camera? 3. Can FPT 360 WiFi also protect my connected IoT devices like my doorbell or camera?

Yes. FPT 360 WiFi monitors all WiFi connected devices in your home and will quarantine and notify you of any suspicious behavior by any of the connected devices in your home.

4. Is setup really that easy? 4. Is setup really that easy?

Yes - simply plug your first pod (via ethernet cable provided) into your existing hub. Then distribute your second pod—and any other pods you have purchased—around the house. Download the FPT 360 WiFi app to label your pods, so you know where they are in your home (e.g., Kitchen, Hallway, etc.).
